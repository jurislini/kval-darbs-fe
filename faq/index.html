FAQ


What is this website about?

This website is designed to help you manage your tasks and stay organized. You can create reminders, set deadlines, and track your progress.



How do I create a new task?

To create a new task, go to the main page and click on the "Add Task" button. Fill in the task details and save it.



How can I edit or delete a task?

You can edit or delete a task by clicking on the task from your task list. There, you will find options to edit or delete it.



Who can I contact for support?

For any support-related queries, you can contact our support team via email at support@example.com.
